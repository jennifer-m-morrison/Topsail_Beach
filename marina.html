Marina Information

Conveniently situated in the heart of Topsail Beach's downtown district, Bush Marina offers a boat ramp and boat slips available as nightly, weekly, or monthly rentals. Parking is available but limited, with clearly marked spaces. Illegally parked vehicles and trailers may be subject to towing at the owner's expense. Please note that parking is allocated to one vehicle per boat launched or leased boat slip, based on available space. For additional truck and trailer parking, spaces are accessible on the ocean side of the water tower, although vehicle entry is restricted between 1 am and 5 am.
