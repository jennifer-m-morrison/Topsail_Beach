The Unique History of Topsail

Located at the southern tip of Topsail Island, just off the southeastern coast of North Carolina, Topsail Beach is the smallest of the island's three towns, which stretches over 26 miles. Topsail Island boasts a rich and diverse history, and this coastal community is conveniently positioned off US Highway 17, roughly halfway between Wilmington and Jacksonville. Legend has it that the island earned its name, pronounced as "Tops'l," because pirate ships would seek refuge behind it, with their topsails visible from the open sea.
